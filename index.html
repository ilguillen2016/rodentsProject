Guinea Pigs aren't from Papua New Guinea

Much to everyone's suprise, Guinea Pigs don't hail from Paupa New Guinea, they are actually from the Peruvian Andes. The Quechua word for guinea pig is cuye or sometimes cuy. These furry creatures are a delicacy in many indgenous cultures in South America.
